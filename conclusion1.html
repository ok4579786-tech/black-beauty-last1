Effectiveness of the Book

Evaluation, recommendation, and supporting sources

Black Beauty is an effective novel that promotes awareness of animal welfare through a clear and emotionally engaging narrative. Because the story is told from the horse’s point of view, readers understand how kindness, neglect, and cruelty affect an animal’s life. This approach makes the message personal and easier to remember.

The novel is recommended for Senior High School students because it is readable, organized, and socially relevant. It encourages empathy and ethical thinking by showing that animals depend on human choices. The themes are still applicable today, especially when discussing responsible care and welfare standards for horses.
